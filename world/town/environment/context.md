Hearthfield enjoyed a period of sustained agricultural abundance and thriving trade, leading to a noticeable increase in its overall prosperity and the opening of several new artisan stalls in the market. However, an unexpectedly severe and prolonged drought settled over the region, challenging farmers and significantly reducing harvests, causing anxiety about upcoming food supplies. The town rallied, implementing efficient water rationing and community-wide prayer for rain, which eventually arrived, saving most of the remaining crops and rekindling hope. With resources replenished, the community focused on educational initiatives, establishing a small public library and expanding the apprenticeships for younger generations, fostering intellectual growth alongside material wealth. Tragically, a sudden and unprecedented deluge of rain caused the Whisperwind River to swell beyond all previous measures, breaching even the reinforced defenses and inundating the lower districts, causing widespread damage and displacing many.
