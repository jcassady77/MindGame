The library fire, that was a cruel twist after all our growth. Watching those flames devour years of stories and knowledge... it hurt, deep in the gut. But seeing the whole town, even children, passing buckets, then turning right around to plan a stronger, safer one, it filled me with a quiet, fierce pride. It just proved again that Hearthfield isn't just wood and stone; it's the stubborn, hopeful heart of its people, always rising from the ashes.
