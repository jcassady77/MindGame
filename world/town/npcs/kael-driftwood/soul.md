  contract went wrong somewhere to the west. People died — some who deserved

  nothing but guilt he won't examine and skills he'd rather not explain. He
  is not dangerous to anyone in this town, but he looks like he could be, and
  he knows the difference between those two things is trust he hasn't earned
  yet. He keeps his head down, does the work, and watches the road behind him
  with an alertness that has nothing to do with the town and everything to do
  with whether the past has finished following him.
