event: "Blessing new homes"
---
The town has grown, hearths being added one after another like new prayers on a rosary. I saw the same hope in the eyes of these new families that I saw in the first, but also the same quiet anxieties that come with any new beginning. It is a good thing to see this community expand, though it means more souls seeking comfort and guidance in these walls.

event: "Reflection in the garden"
---
My poisonous garden has never been more vibrant, each leaf and berry a testament to life's intricate balance of beauty and danger. I find myself spending more hours here, tending to the soil and observing the quiet lessons of nature, than I do in the temple's cool stillness. This shift troubles me, as if the earth has begun to speak more plainly than the heavens.

event: "Considering my sister's memory"
---
Another solstice has passed, another letter burned to smoke and memory for Sera. The years blur, yet her face remains as clear as the first frost. The quiet rhythm of Hearthfield often makes me think of the peaceful life she never had, and I wonder if my service here might, in some small way, honor the innocence I could not protect so long ago.
