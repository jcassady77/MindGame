I spent weeks observing the new settlers, hoping their novel approaches to building or farming might reveal some overlooked principle that could unstick my own research. Their methods were efficient, even clever, but ultimately, they were about the earth and tools, not the hidden currents of the aether. It only reinforced how profoundly different my path is from theirs.
